Nepal’s Premium Aquarium Paradise

Founded and owned by Ashish Singh Dangol, Pan’s Pets Paradise has become Kathmandu’s most trusted source for exotic freshwater fish since opening its doors in Jyatha, Thamel.

We specialize in healthy, vibrant imports — from stunning Flowerhorns and majestic Asian Arowanas to rare Cichlids, Bettas, and fully planted tanks. Every fish is quarantined and cared for with passion.

Our showroom is an underwater wonderland — come experience the lush planted aquariums and professional setups that make our store truly unique in Nepal.
